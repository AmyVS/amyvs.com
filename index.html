About

Computers and technology have always been a central part of my life, from drawing shapes with a Logo turtle on my Apple IIe to teaching myself HTML, Markdown, and CSS in order to help manage site content for my last two music tech jobs (Discogs and Bandcamp). In July of 2014, I was accepted into Epicodus, an intensive four month, forty plus hours per week, in person programming class. I am now a full-stack Ruby on Rails developer with the ability to pick up a brand new language or tool and have it feel like an old friend in as little as a week's time.


Other fun facts about me: I have been a professional French Horn player for numerous years, and am still gigging. When I am not coding or playing music, you can find me swinging on a highflying trapeze, kiteboarding down the wild Columbia river, backpacking in some remote location, or plotting my next adventure.
